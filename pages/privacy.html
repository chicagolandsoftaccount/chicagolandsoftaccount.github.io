Privacy Policy

This site uses cookies. It uses a cookie to keep track of your GDPR decision, as well as tracking with Google Analytics to learn about who is visiting the site. The share buttons in the sidebar also use cookies. For pages with Disqus comments, those also use cookies from Disqus. The GDPR is EU legislation, but I am in the USA. Even so, I still have the GDPR notification. If you do not want to be tracked or use cookies, please do not use this site.

It's 2020. Websites track users, at least to some degree. It's not as invasive as you might think though. And even if you block cookies and use something like uBlock Origin to block tracking-related domains, a website owner can still just check through their server logs to see the requests you've made to the server. Any website owner can see what their users are doing on their website, see their user agent (OS/browser/etc)... if they really want to. But it's honestly not a big deal.
